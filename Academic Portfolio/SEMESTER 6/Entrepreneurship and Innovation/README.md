### ULRS3032 – Entrepreneurship and Innovation

This course develops entrepreneurial skills through business model creation, innovation, and real business execution activities. Students work on pitching, marketing, and business operations.

#### Key Topics Learned
- Entrepreneurship Fundamentals
- Business Model Canvas (BMC)
- Innovation & Creativity
- AI-assisted Business Ideas
- Business Execution & Marketing
- Sales & Financial Reporting

#### Skills Acquired
- Entrepreneurial Thinking
- Business Planning
- Communication & Pitching
- Marketing Strategy
- Leadership & Team Management

# 🎓 Semester 6 (2025/2026 Session 2)

Semester 6 focuses on advanced data engineering, business intelligence, high-performance computing, and real-world industry applications. Students are exposed to industrial projects, work-based learning, cloud tools, BI systems, and entrepreneurship activities to strengthen technical, analytical, and professional skills.

## 📚 Courses Taken

---

### SECP3843 – Special Topic in Data Engineering

This course explores emerging trends, tools, and industry practices in data engineering. It emphasizes big data technologies, modern data systems, and real-world applications through industry engagement and project-based learning.

---

### SECP3106 – Application Development (WBL)

This Work-Based Learning course focuses on developing real-world applications through industry exposure and practical implementation. Students apply software development concepts in a professional environment.

---

### SECP3133 – High Performance Data Processing

This course introduces cloud computing, HPC concepts, and large-scale data processing techniques. It focuses on distributed systems and modern computing frameworks for big data applications.

---

### SECP3213 – Business Intelligence

This course covers business intelligence concepts, data warehousing, ETL processes, and data visualization using industry tools such as Alteryx, Tableau, and Power BI.

---

### ULRS3032 – Entrepreneurship and Innovation

This course develops entrepreneurial skills through business model creation, innovation, and real business execution activities. Students work on pitching, marketing, and business operations.

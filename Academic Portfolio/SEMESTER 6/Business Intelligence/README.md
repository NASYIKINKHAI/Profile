### SECP3213 – Business Intelligence

This course covers business intelligence concepts, data warehousing, ETL processes, and data visualization using industry tools such as Alteryx.

#### Key Topics Learned
- Business Intelligence Fundamentals
- Data Warehouse & ETL
- Dashboard Design
- Data Visualization
- Predictive Analytics
- BI Tools (Alteryx)

#### Skills Acquired
- Data Visualization Skills
- BI Tool Proficiency
- Data Storytelling
- Analytical Thinking
- Decision Support Systems

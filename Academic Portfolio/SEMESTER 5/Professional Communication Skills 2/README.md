### UHLB3132 / UHLB2122 – Professional Communication Skills 2

This course develops advanced professional communication skills, focusing on proposal writing, presentations, and formal group discussions at B2 level proficiency.

#### Key Topics Learned
- Business Proposal Writing
- Oral Presentation Skills
- Group Discussion Techniques
- Argument Development
- Professional Communication Strategies

#### Skills Acquired
- Business Communication
- Public Speaking
- Critical Thinking & Argumentation
- Team Collaboration
- Academic & Professional Writing

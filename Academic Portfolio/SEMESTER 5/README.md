# 🎓 Semester 5 (2025/2026 Session 1)

Semester 5 focuses on strengthening core foundations in Artificial Intelligence, database systems, enterprise systems, communication, and interdisciplinary knowledge. The semester emphasizes problem-solving, system design, and real-world application of computing technologies through lectures, labs, and project-based learning.

## 📚 Courses Taken

---

### SECJ3553 – Artificial Intelligence

This foundational course introduces students to Artificial Intelligence concepts, including search algorithms, knowledge representation, and machine learning techniques. It combines theoretical understanding with practical applications in real-world AI problems.

---

### SECP3623 – Database Programming

This course enhances advanced database skills focusing on both relational (SQL) and non-relational (NoSQL) systems. Students learn scalable database design, querying techniques, and integration of database systems in applications.

---

### SCSP3744 – Enterprise System Design and Modeling

This course focuses on enterprise information systems, system integration, and enterprise architecture frameworks. It emphasizes real-world business process modeling and enterprise system design.

---

### SKEB3503 / SKEF3503 – Physiology and Introduction to Medicine

This interdisciplinary course introduces students to human physiology and basic medical concepts, focusing on how body systems maintain homeostasis and interact in medical contexts.

---

### UHLB3132 / UHLB2122 – Professional Communication Skills 2

This course develops advanced professional communication skills, focusing on proposal writing, presentations, and formal group discussions at B2 level proficiency.

---

### UHLC1112 – Mandarin Language I

This introductory language course builds basic Mandarin communication skills, focusing on speaking, reading, and writing fundamentals for everyday and academic use.

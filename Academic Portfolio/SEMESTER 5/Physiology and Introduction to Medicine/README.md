### SKEB3503 / SKEF3503 – Physiology and Introduction to Medicine

This interdisciplinary course introduces students to human physiology and basic medical concepts, focusing on how body systems maintain homeostasis and interact in medical contexts.

#### Key Topics Learned
- Human Body Systems
- Physiology & Anatomy Basics
- Homeostasis Mechanisms
- Common Physiological Disorders
- Biomedical System Interactions
- Introduction to Medical Terminology

#### Skills Acquired
- Basic Medical Understanding
- Analytical Thinking in Biomedical Context
- Scientific Reasoning

## 📖 Overview

The industry talk focused on system development processes and real-world practices in the technology industry. The session was delivered by **Ms. Qistina Batrisyia Binti Azman Shah**, Professional in AI Operation at Credence.

System development was explained as a structured process that involves analysis, design, programming, testing, implementation, and maintenance. Each phase plays an important role in ensuring that information systems are efficient, reliable, and meet organizational requirements.

Credence, a subsidiary of Telekom Malaysia (TM), is a digital services company that focuses on cloud advisory, migration, SaaS, analytics, and application modernization. The company provides end-to-end digital solutions from infrastructure to data insights, supporting both enterprise and public sector digital transformation.

## 💡 Skills Required for Data Engineers

The session highlighted essential skills required for future data engineers:

### Technical Skills
- Programming in SQL, Python, Java, and R
- Data modeling techniques (Snowflake schema, etc.)
- Big data technologies such as Hadoop
- Data ingestion and pipeline management
- Tools such as Apache Kafka and data processing frameworks

### Soft Skills
- Communication skills
- Teamwork and collaboration
- Critical thinking
- Problem-solving ability
- Time management

These skills are important for building efficient data pipelines, handling large-scale data, and ensuring smooth system operations in real-world environments.

---

## 💭 Reflection

The session provided a clear understanding of how system development is applied in real industrial environments. Exposure to Credence’s technologies and workflows highlighted the importance of both technical and soft skills in the field of Data Engineering.

Continuous learning is essential to keep up with rapidly evolving technologies such as cloud computing, big data, and artificial intelligence. Strong programming knowledge combined with effective communication and teamwork skills is necessary to succeed in the industry.

Awareness of industry tools such as PostgreSQL, ClickHouse, and Apache Airflow demonstrated how data pipelines are built and maintained in professional environments. This knowledge is valuable for future career development in system development and data engineering roles.

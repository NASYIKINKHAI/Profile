## 📖 Overview

The industrial talk provided an in-depth overview of how PPG Industries utilizes SAP (Systems, Applications, and Products in Data Processing) as an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system to manage global business operations.

The SAP system has been implemented in PPG since 1991 and has evolved from SAP R/3 and ECC to the modern SAP S/4HANA platform. This evolution demonstrates continuous digital transformation in supporting efficient, real-time enterprise operations.

The system integrates multiple business processes across global branches, ensuring consistency and efficiency in operations such as procurement, production planning, warehouse management, and sales.

### Key SAP Business Streams
- Procure to Pay (PTP)
- Demand to Replenish (DTR)
- Order to Cash (OTC)
- Record to Report (RTR)

### SAP Module Structure
- **Functional Modules** – Support business operations and processes
- **Technical Modules** – System development, customization, and architecture management
- **Reporting Modules** – Business Warehouse (BW), SAP Business Intelligence (BI), SAP Analytics Cloud (SAC)

The session also introduced the **Connect PPG initiative**, a global digital transformation project aimed at standardizing and streamlining operations across different regions.

A comparison between SAP ECC and SAP S/4HANA was discussed, highlighting that S/4HANA is a more advanced system built on in-memory database technology, enabling faster and real-time data processing.

## 💭 Reflection

The session provided valuable insight into how large-scale organizations manage complex business processes through integrated ERP systems. SAP plays a critical role in ensuring operational efficiency, data consistency, and real-time decision-making across global branches.

Understanding the integration of multiple SAP modules demonstrated how different departments rely on a centralized system to maintain smooth workflows. This highlights the importance of system design, database management, and process automation in modern enterprises.

The discussion on SAP S/4HANA emphasized the importance of technological advancement in enterprise systems, particularly in improving speed, efficiency, and scalability.

Overall, the talk enhanced awareness of how digital transformation impacts organizational success and highlighted the importance of continuous learning and adaptability in pursuing careers within the technology and data fields.

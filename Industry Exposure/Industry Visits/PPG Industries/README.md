## 📖 Overview

The industry visit to PPG Digital IT Malaysia Technology Center provided valuable exposure to how data and digital technologies support global business operations. The session introduced the role of Data Engineering, SAP Operations, and Analytics in managing enterprise-scale processes and enabling data-driven decision-making.

Participants were given insights into PPG’s data ecosystem, where data from multiple sources is integrated and processed to support business operations. The sharing session also highlighted the importance of collaboration between business and technical teams in delivering effective data solutions.

A key highlight of the visit was the discussion on the Recoverable Assets (RA) and Magna Carta (MC) case study, which demonstrated how data can be utilized to identify operational challenges, manage inventory, assess financial risks, and support strategic business decisions.

## 🏢 About PPG

PPG Industries is a global leader in paints, coatings, and specialty materials, serving customers in various industries worldwide. Through its Digital IT Malaysia Technology Center, PPG supports global business operations by leveraging modern technologies, data platforms, and digital transformation initiatives to improve efficiency and innovation.

The organization continuously invests in technology-driven solutions to enhance business performance and deliver value across its global operations.

## 💭 Reflection

The industry visit provided valuable insights into how data engineering and enterprise systems are applied in real-world business environments. The session demonstrated that effective data solutions require not only technical expertise but also a strong understanding of business processes and operational requirements.

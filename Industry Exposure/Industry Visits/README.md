# 🏭 Industry Visits

This section documents industry visits attended throughout the Bachelor of Computer Science (Data Engineering) programme at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM).

Industry visits provide valuable opportunities to observe real-world business operations, industry technologies, data-driven processes, and workplace environments. These experiences help bridge academic learning with practical applications while enhancing understanding of industry expectations and professional practices.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Gain exposure to real industrial environments
- Understand industry workflows and technologies
- Learn about digital transformation initiatives
- Explore career opportunities in technology and data-related fields
- Connect academic knowledge with practical industry applications

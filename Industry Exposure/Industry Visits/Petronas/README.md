## 📖 Overview

The industry visit to PETRONAS provided valuable exposure to one of Malaysia's largest multinational corporations and its extensive use of technology to support business operations. The session offered insights into how technology and information systems contribute to organizational efficiency, communication, and decision-making processes within the oil and gas industry.

The sharing session highlighted the importance of digital transformation, software engineering, and data connectivity in managing large-scale business operations. As a global organization, PETRONAS collaborates with partners and stakeholders from various countries, requiring reliable information systems and strong technological infrastructure to ensure seamless operations.

The visit also included discussions on emerging technologies and future workforce demands within the technology sector.

---

## 🏢 About PETRONAS

PETRONAS (Petroliam Nasional Berhad) is Malaysia’s national oil and gas corporation and one of the largest multinational companies in Southeast Asia. The company operates across the entire energy value chain, including exploration, production, refining, marketing, and renewable energy initiatives.

PETRONAS continuously invests in technological innovation and digital transformation to improve operational efficiency, business sustainability, and global competitiveness.
